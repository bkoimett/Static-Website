We offer both long-term and short-term treatment. The long-term treatment is residential and involves individual counselling, family therapy, group therapy, a 12-step program, and spiritual counselling. We also offer outpatient services depending on clients’ preferences and needs.
One may be addicted if they lack the willpower to control their substance use or behaviors. At the point when addiction controls someone instead of them controlling it, professional treatment is recommended.
Family and friends play a crucial role in motivating individuals with drug problems to seek and stay in treatment. Family therapy can be vital, strengthening and extending treatment benefits.
Reduce stigma and raise awareness, as some may not realize they have a problem or how to get help. Treatment does not need to be voluntary to be effective. Family, friends, professionals, or institutions can initiate treatment.
We treat men and women, young and old, suffering from drug addiction, behavioral addictions, and mental health disorders.
