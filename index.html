Mission

Our Programs

We offer tailor-made programs ensuring our clients heal, recover, and are empowered — with the BEST RATES in Kenya!

INPATIENT PROGRAMME

The inpatient programme helps individuals suffering from substance abuse through structured treatment away from usual work or home environments. Drug testing is performed upon arrival to tailor personalized care.

OUR MODEL

We utilize an eclectic model for whole-person recovery combining the 12 Steps, therapeutic community, cognitive-behavioural therapy, rational emotive therapy, and occupational therapy.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

We believe addiction is treatable and manageable. With timely diagnosis and proper care, individuals can recover, regain function, and lead productive lives in the community.
